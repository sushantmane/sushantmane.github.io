About Me
I am Sushant Mane, a Computer Science student at Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli. I'm web developer having special interest in Open Source. Since May 2014, I'm working as President of Walchand Linux Users' Group. Web devlopment & design, Programming, Operating Systems, IoT(Internet of Things), Big data and Hadoop are some of my favourite topics.
sushant@WLUG

The most important part of my four years of engineering started with responsibility of President of Walchand Linux Users' Group(WLUG). Walchand Linux Users' Group popularly known as WLUG CLub is group of open source enthusiast/mavericks. Since its inception in 2003 WLUG is working for spreading the awareness about Open Source. Every year we have been organizing 5-7 open source events in Western Maharashtra(IN). And weekly meet-ups on every Wednesday to promote free and open source software/technologies.

Since Jul. 2012, I'm associated with WLUG. I was Assistant Web Developer during 2013-2014. I have been part of organizing committee of state level Metamorphosis and Techno-Tweet workshops. In Metamorphosis-2K13 I had conducted hands-on session on 'Introduction to Python Programming'. In April 2014 I got selected as President. And the journey continues . . .
